Overview
The first VibeCoding competition judged completely by AI! No slides, no pitches, just your live URL and an AI user experience testing agent.

Welcome to The VIbeCode Olympics!


Do you love vibecoding? Can you generate and deploy an app in two hours? Would you love to show the world that Boston has some of the coolest and smartest web builders out there?

If the answer to any of these is yes, then you're invited to the first ever VibeCode Olympics, judged completely by AI.



AlcivarTech has created an AI User Experience testing agent that can navigate your website with scrolls and clicks just like a real user. Give it a goal and see if your website is built well enough for it to make it work. It's about as attentive and motivated as your average unfamiliar user, so you'll have to make sure to stick to the best user experience principles: A single call to action, clear user stories, and clear flows through your application.

All participants will have access to the AI Judging Agent before the competition so you can familiarize yourself with how it works. You can check out the github right here: https://github.com/mindfulcoder49/AIUXTester
